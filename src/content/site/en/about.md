My nickname is Kele, which is Chinese pinyin of the word "cola". I got this name because I liked drinking Coca-Cola when I was younger. I majored in Mechanical Engineering and have years of work experience in FPGA, digital signal processing, and communication system design. I write this blog to share some of my knowledge and thoughts, and most importantly, for fun.

I write the content of this blog in [Markdown](https://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/), build the site with [Astro](https://astro.build/), and deploy it to [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/).

The domain [kele14.com](https://kele14.com) is registered through [Google Domains](https://domains.google/), and the static content is hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/kele14x/kele14x.github.io).

You can find me on [twitter](https://twitter.com/kele_plus).
